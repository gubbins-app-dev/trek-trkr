While the App does not collect any personally identifiable information directly, it uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain data automatically, including:

This data collection is performed solely by Google. For information on how Google uses this data, see their policies:

Google Privacy & Terms – Advertising

2. Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or account details. The App has no account system and requires no registration.

Other than data processed by Google AdMob, the App does not collect:

  • Custom device metrics

We do not collect or share any data ourselves. Google may collect and process data for:

  • Ad delivery

Gubbins App Development does not access or control this data.

5. Advertising and User Choices

Ads in the App are provided by Google AdMob. Users can manage ad tracking and preferences via:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking

If you disable tracking, ads may still appear but will not be personalised.

6. Data Security

We do not collect or store personal data, so there are no risks related to handling such data. Google AdMob processes its own data under its security standards. Users should protect their devices with standard precautions.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. AdMob is configured to comply with child-appropriate policies when applicable.
