Privacy Policy for “Trek Trkr” App

Effective Date: May 1st, 2025

Gubbins App Development (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application (“App”). By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following data when you use the App:

  • Location Data: We collect your device’s location in the background to provide core functionality, even when the app is closed or not in use.
  • Battery Information: We collect your device’s battery level and charging status to help optimise app performance.
  • User Information: When you sign in, we collect basic account details (such as email address and user ID) via Firebase Authentication.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Monitor and store your location and battery information securely.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.
  • Back up data securely to the cloud via Firebase.
  • Notify trusted contacts in special cases (e.g., SOS alerts).
  • Support internal analytics for service improvements.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party marketers.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which follows industry-standard security practices to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

4. User Controls

You have control over your data within the app:

  • You can manually delete all stored location, battery, and account data via the app at any time.
  • You can disable location access through your device settings (which may limit app features).
  • You can request additional data assistance by contacting us at gubbins.app.dev@icloud.com.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore Database, Cloud Messaging). Firebase may collect basic anonymized analytics as described in their privacy documentation.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the Effective Date above and may notify you through the app.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at gubbins.app.dev@icloud.com.
